about

Grace Ryan is a creative professional and millenial know-it-all with a background in content and digital media. She has been the go-to family tech support since the tender age of 10, when her mom retired from the position after saying, "I'm not fixing that computer again. Just get Grace to do it." And a legend was born.

Grace received her B.A. in English and Theatre from the University of South Carolina in 2015, and since then has held a number of random jobs while creating and writing for her personal website, The Loosely Bound. When she's not serving eggs to local businessmen while wearing a button-down shirt, you can find Grace reviewing books, learning JavaScript, and freelancing.

Keep your small dogs away from her - she will steal them.
